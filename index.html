About This Project

This toolkit was created by a student to help teens manage stress, anxiety, and emotional well-being using simple, practical techniques and easy-to-access resources.

Toolkit Sections

Breathing Exercises
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat 4 times.
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale slowly for 8. Helps reduce anxiety.
  • Deep Belly Breathing: Breathe deeply into your belly, feeling it rise and fall, to relax your body.
Study Stress Tips
  • Break study time into focused chunks (e.g., 25 minutes studying, 5-minute break) — try the Pomodoro Technique.
  • Set small, achievable goals for each session to avoid feeling overwhelmed.
  • Stay hydrated and take short physical breaks to refresh your mind.
  • Practice positive self-talk — remind yourself that effort matters more than perfection.
  • Get enough sleep to help your brain process and remember information better.
Screen-Time Detox Plan
  • Set daily screen time limits for non-essential use (e.g., social media).
  • Use “Do Not Disturb” mode during study or before bed to avoid distractions.
  • Take “digital detox breaks” — at least 30 minutes away from screens every few hours.
  • Replace screen time with relaxing activities like reading, journaling, or walking.
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime to improve sleep quality.
